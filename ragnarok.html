Ragnarok Conspiracy

This project started out with research for my novel Ragnarok Conspiracy. If you want to read some fiction that was inspired by the idea of a quantum blockchain heist, you should read Ragnarok Conspiracy. While completely fiction, it should be a great read for anyone interested in the subject. The book is currently available for free in both the English and Spanish edition on most e-book outlets.
